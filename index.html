Our Story

Crafting Outdoor Elegance One Yard at a Time

Elegance in Green 502 Landscaping Service INC was built on a simple belief: every outdoor space deserves to be beautiful, functional, and alive. Based in Opa-Locka, FL, we bring passion, craftsmanship, and deep horticultural knowledge to every project we touch.

From sweeping custom landscape designs to precision drainage solutions, our team approaches every property as a unique canvas — listening to your vision and turning it into a lush reality that lasts for years.
